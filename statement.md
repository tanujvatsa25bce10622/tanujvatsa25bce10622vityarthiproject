**Problem Statement:**

Schools and Colleges often use manual registers or paper lists to track student data and attendance, which can lead to errors and wasted time. Quick, accurate access to student details and attendance is important for teachers and administrators. This project aims to digitally manage student records and attendance for easier, faster, and more reliable tracking.

**Who Can Use This Program:**

-School teachers
-College professors
-Tutoring centers
-Any educational institution

**Main Features**

-Takes student marks and attendance
-Checks if student can take exams (attendance rule)
-Calculates total score
-Gives grades based on class performance
-Shows clear results for each student
